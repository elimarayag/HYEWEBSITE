ABOUT US

Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises was founded on 2003, initially started with selling of electronics equipment such as DVD players, and speakers as main products. A year after operation in Florentino Torres St. Santa Cruz Manila (2004), Mr. Sy took over the top management of Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises.

Through the successful years of business, Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises which started as a single proprietorship turned into a corporation last 2011, and named Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises Corporation. At present the company has affiliated companies nationwide, from Aklan, Butuan, Cagayan De Oro, Cebu, and Davao as the provincial branches.

Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises Corporation is an appointed distributor and sub-distributor of many reputable brands in the Philippines, such as Hikvision, Honeywell, Ruijie/Reyee, ITC, IMOU, EZVIZ, ZKTeco, and many others. We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality security products and solutions that meet their specific needs.
