About Us

Through the successful years of business, Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises which started as a single proprietorship turned into a corporation last 2011, and named Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises Corporation.

At present the company has affiliated companies nationwide, from Aklan, Butuan, Cagayan De Oro, Cebu City, and Davao City as the provincial branches.

Hua Yang Electronics Enterprises Corporation is an appointed distributor and sub-distributor of many reputable brands in the Philippines, such as Hikvision, Honeywell, Ruijie/Reyee, ITC, IMOU, EZVIZ, ZKTeco, and many others.

We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality security products and solutions that meet their specific needs.
